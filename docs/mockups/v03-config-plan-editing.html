AI Parse Granularity Picker

Quick pick for choosing how finely the AI should break down the plan into phases.

Coarse — 3-5 phases High-level phases. Good for small tasks or quick iterations.
Medium — 5-10 phases (default) Balanced breakdown. Each phase is a meaningful unit of work.
Fine — 10-20 phases Granular phases. Best for complex tasks requiring careful monitoring.
